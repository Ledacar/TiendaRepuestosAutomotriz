About Automotive S&L

We are a company dedicated to the sale of auto parts. Founded in 1998 in the city of Mazatlan Sinaloa, Mexico We sell spare parts for cars, motorcycles, vans, trucks, etc. We have from the most basic spare parts to oil changes, box changes, sale of lubricants, everything that goes with vehicles.

We are a company specializing in the sale of spare parts for new and used cars, accessories, preventive and corrective maintenance, and bodywork and painting. Have highly qualified personnel, state-of-the-art technology and infrastructure that always guarantee a comprehensive service of the highest quality.
